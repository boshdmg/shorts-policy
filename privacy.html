Privacy Policy

Shorts? — Last updated April 25, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Shorts? app handles your information.

What we collect

Shorts? collects your device's location to fetch a weather forecast. Your location is sent directly to OpenWeatherMap to retrieve local weather data. It is not stored, logged, or shared with anyone else.

Shorts? uses Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data, including app opens, screens viewed, and basic device information such as OS version and device type. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you. You can learn more about how Google handles this data at policies.google.com/privacy.

What we don't collect

  • No account or registration information
  • No name, email address, or contact details
  • No payment information
  • No precise location history — location is requested in the moment and not retained

Third parties

The only third-party services used are:

  • OpenWeatherMap — receives your location to return a weather forecast. Their privacy policy.
  • Google Firebase Analytics — receives anonymous usage events. Google's privacy policy.

Children

Shorts? is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect the change.
